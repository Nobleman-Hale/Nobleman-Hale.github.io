Analysis of various websites

Arngen.net

Arngren appears to be a site for buying various things, though primarily motorbikes in Norway. I can't figure much else out due to the language barrier unfortunatley.

  • Contrast: Color is all over the place, stark white mixed with blue green and red. None of those colors provide decent information.
  • Repetition: Every box is a different size, I can't even begin to understand what box means what on this site, and some products don't even have boxes!
  • Aligment: See previous about boxes. It's more like a collage than a storefront.
  • Proximity: Everything is close together, meaning I can't tell what is and isnt' related.

Virgin America

Virgin Atlantic is a company dedicated to travel around the globe. Their website seems to follow some fantastic design convention.

  • Contrast: Color looks great! It uses contrasting colors in addition to the Virgin Atlantic brand colors to make a soft website
  • Repetition: Font colors are reused throughout the website, genuinely looks amazing. Fonts are all decently professional as well.
  • Aligment: Everythinhg is centered and stacks up nicely
  • Proximity: Things in the various stacked boxes on this webpage are all close together, making them easy to ready in addition to the affromentioned colored boxes.
